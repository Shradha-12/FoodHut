How to create your acccount

1. Head to FoodHut

2. Click Sign in & Register in the upper right corner

3. In the Enter a location box, enter your full address including building number, street address, city, state, and zip code.

4. A drop-down will populate with the matching address below the “Enter a location” box, select the matching address.

5. Then click the Check My Address button.

6. Once your address is confirmed , select confirm.

7. When you’ve got everything you want, click Check Out

8. Enter an email address for your account, this should be one where you can receive updates about your box and tracking information. Feel free to opt into our newsletter!

9. Then click Continue as Guest

10. Enter your delivery address and contact information.

11. Then click Continue

12. Check Yes, I agree with the terms and conditions.
