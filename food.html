Who are we?

It is a centralised network that connects the government, non-government organisations, private contributors, volunteers, and those in need across India to facilitate the distribution of food and humanitarian relief.

What are we doing?

Our business provides a platform for horeca owners, farmers, and wholesalers to sell unsold raw food and leftover food to nearby retailers. The restaurant owners will donate freshly prepared food to nursing homes, orphanages, and nonprofit organisations in the area. Locals should dump their expired food at the surrounding compost piles. Additionally, supermarkets are permitted to donate nearly-expired baked items to charitable organisations for immediate consumption.
